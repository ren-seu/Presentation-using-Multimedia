What is the wireless card? The wireless network card is a wireless terminal device, which is used to connect to the Internet through a wireless connection within the wireless coverage of a wireless local area network. Generally speaking, a wireless card is a device that does not need to be connected to a network cable, and a wireless network card is integrated with a general-purpose laptop. Therefore, the laptop can surf the Internet wirelessly like a mobile phone, as long as there is a wireless network signal in the air in the area. There are two types of wireless cards one is PCI wireless network card or a USB wireless network card, and the other is a 3G wireless network card similar to U disk. The former is mainly suitable for installing wireless network cards. Desktop computer, its network signal is limited, and the principle of the notebook wireless network card is the same, it can accept wireless network signals, such as wireless router wireless network and hotspot WIFI, etc.
